Who We Are


The SMS Higher Education Group helps students find the best colleges and universities in the UK. SMS Higher Education Group also assists students in finding part-time work to help them meet their daily expenses. SMS Higher Group will handle all of your UK student visa processing hassles.

Why Choose SMS


SMS Higher Education Group is a UK-based education consultancy group, and its main target is to open the door for students to fulfil their dream to study in the UK. The company believes that each student needs professional guidance and accurate pieces of information about every aspect of studying abroad.

Our Services


The SMS Higher Education Group offers various services to individuals and partners. We assist not just students but also large educational institutions and universities. SMS Higher Education Group has earned the trust of students and universities through many years of service.
